What does ANTWALLET do?

ANTWALLET is an online and mobile market place where customers can find attractive deals that local merchants offer. It offers a platform to connect customers and merchants where both benefit from each other in a variety of ways. Customer can find amazing offers to save on instant buys, subscribe to plans with term payments that’s affordable and cherish one’s dreams.

Earning money is difficult but you can spend it wisely, and this is very important in current economic conditions and that’s where we serve you, benefiting customers and merchants in the eco-system.

Our Vision is to make our customers happy by spending smart and ensure they realize their long-wished goals!

Our Mission

To provide deal-seeking Canadians and their families with discounts on high quality products and services from reputable merchants. ANTWALLET strives to be the most respected deal company in the industry and aims to accomplish this through superior customer service, careful selection of partners, a unique combination of local businesses and large brand names, and a friendly user experience for both our customers and business partners.
