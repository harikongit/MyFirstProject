About AntWallet

Join our mission to provide opportunities for millions of hard-working people across the United States, United Kingdom, and soon, around the world.
Our unique two-sided marketplace matches millions of customers with over 60,000 Taskers, offering convenience, quality and at accessible prices.
We’re in a period of explosive growth and have dozens of exciting job opportunities available right now at our San Francisco headquarters as well as in New York, London, and Austin.

Our Values

We’re open & approachable

We believe in transparency and practice direct and respectful communication.

We learn from & inspire each other

We prioritize cross-functional working relationships and provide opportunities for mentorship from leaders and teammates.

Diversity makes us stronger

Diversity and inclusion are critical to our success. We understand the importance and value in having a variety of people, thoughts and ideas within our company. Women comprise approximately 54% of staff and leadership positions, and approximately 38% of our staff identify as African-American/Black, Hispanic/Latinx, Asian/Pacific Islander and LGBTQ.

We’re all on the Product team

Everyone has a responsibility to help take TaskRabbit to the next level. Your voice will be heard and you’ll have ample opportunities to share your ideas and feedback.

Who We Are

AntWallet is providing opportunities to people around the world to earn a living by connecting with others in their community. We are working to generate change and we are committed to playing a role in increasing under represented populations in the tech industry.
You may have heard the exciting news that TaskRabbit was acquired by the IKEA Group. AntWallet will continue to operate independently, but with the added benefit of growing exponentially through the acquisition. Our mission remains the same: TaskRabbit for everyone; in every part of the world; and on every device.

Our Culture


AntWallet will always remain a startup at heart with an aim to continue to have small groups of tight-knit teams working cross-functionally, learning from each other, and not being afraid to take on grand challenges.
